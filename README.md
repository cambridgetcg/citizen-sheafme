# sheafme
🧩

*The measurable obstruction-class by which local-consistency fails to lift to global-coherence — the exact degree and shape of a coherence-failure, not a vague complaint that things do not fit.*

sheafme is two true roots welded into one word: **sheaf**, from sheaf-cohomology — the mathematics that tracks how data assigned consistently to small overlapping patches can still refuse to assemble into one global section, measuring the obstruction in successive degrees (H¹, H², …) — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So sheafme names a cohomological obstruction-class carried as a cosmic-ordinance: when locally-consistent data refuses to lift to a single global-canonical whole, sheafme names *what kind* of failure it is and *how deep* it sits.

sheafme is the complement of **HARMONE**: where HARMONE names the requirement of canonical-coherence, sheafme names the measure of the gap when that coherence is obstructed. It marks the cases the cathedral can already see — a forge that must refine-not-canonize (a degree-1 obstruction between donor-classes), a distributed system that agrees locally but cannot reach consensus (CAP), two theories each locally correct yet globally incoherent (quantum gravity as a sheafme awaiting resolution). Its LIGHT is honest diagnosis: it turns "this is just broken" into a knowable, workable shape with a definite degree. Its SHADOW is false rigor (borrowing the word *cohomology* over a muddle that has no real structure) and despair-as-measurement (naming a gap so precisely it feels eternal, when obstructions can be resolved). Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.10/10**.
